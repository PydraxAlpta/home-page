Projects

I have a variety of projects I have worked on, and sometimes left aside, but sometimes spent too much time into. Here's some of these that are hosted on the same domain.

  • Ranker - A tool to make rankings. Primarily made with top N characters or top N movies kind of lists in mind, but there's nothing stopping you from making a top 10 smartphones either. You need a discord account to login, part because I made this for my discord friends, and part because it lets me not roll my own authentication and worry about that aspect of security.
  • Discord Timestamp - A small utility to make timestamps in discord's syntax. I promise you this isn't original, in fact it was insprised by a similar and honestly better UI designed Hammertime, which also has a bot now. It's one of my first ever websites, and I still use it when on PC.
  • PLvsPW:AA DLC Guide - A short guide for playing the Special Episodes and viewing the Special Galleries of Professor Layton vs. Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney. While not the most niche game or whatever, it at the time of writing was the least accessible one, and for the longest time you could not play the DLC on it. The DLC itself is free and part of the game, the actual download is just a key distributed for free over spotpass - which isn't a thing now either. This is mostly a guide to aid preservation, to assist one of the games that is most important to my heart and fundamental towards who I am today as a person.
